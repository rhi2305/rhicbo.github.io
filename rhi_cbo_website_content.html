About Us

The Reproductive Health Initiative (RHI) CBO is a grassroots organization based in Homa Bay County, Kenya. We work to promote sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR), prevent gender-based violence (GBV), and foster positive social norms, especially among adolescents, women, and marginalized groups.

Our Work

  • SRHR education and advocacy in schools and communities
  • Training elders and religious leaders as SRHR champions
  • Reducing stigma around abortion care and reproductive justice
  • Strengthening support systems to prevent teenage pregnancy and GBV
